I am best known for my compositions of surreal scenery, nature, and abstract images. Following prestigious residencies at the InterUrban ArtHouse in Downtown Overland Park, Kansas and the Charlotte Street Foundation Studios in Kansas City, Missouri I relocated to a studio space at home. My work has been acquired by the Nerman Museum of Contemporary Art, Emprise Bank, and numerous private collections.

This interview by Julie Denesha for Arts Upload features a detailed description of my process and artwork.
